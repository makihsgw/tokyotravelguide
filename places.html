Where to go in Tokyo

遊びましょう!

Thinking of visiting Tokyo but don't know where to go? Below we have listed three must-see attractions for you to check out!
If you visit any of these places be sure to contact us and let us know how you found it!

English Name Japanese Name About Location
Shibuya Scramble Crossing 渋谷スクランブル交差点　 As photographed above, Shibuya's crossing has gained global fame because of the number of people who use it every day. This landmark is arguably the most popular destination for tourists, and a popular picture spot! Otherwise, check out the Starbucks next to the crossing for higher up view.
Take-shita dori, Harajyuku 原宿竹下通 Find all the coolest Tokyo trends at the crowded Take-shita dori! This crowded street is full on popular Japanese fashion for young women. You'll be sure to find a unique piece of clothing here! Afterwards, head to Omotesando for a more luxurious experience.
Senso-ji, Asakusa 浅草浅草寺 This impressive temple allows you to exeperience Japanese traditional culture within Tokyo! See the jaw-dropping temple and buy traditional souvenirs at one of the stalls near by. Attractions surrounding the temple include kimono photoshoots and kimono rentals. What better way to fully immerse yourself in Japanese culture! (To read more about Kimonos refer to our 'Culture' page!)
